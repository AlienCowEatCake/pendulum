System Description

This programme simulates free damped oscillations of a spring pendulum.

It is possible to change the following parameters of the model:

  • Mass
  • Initial displacement from the equilibrium position
  • Spring constant
  • Damping coefficient

The stopwatch, the ability to change the speed of the experience and the ruler to measure the displacement added to improve the convenience of the experiment.

It is possible to display the following graphs of functions in the programme:

  • Function of displacement from time
  • Function of speed from time
  • Function of energy from time

Model

— damping coefficient,

— spring constant,

— mass,

— initial amplitude,

— displacement,

— time,

— cyclic frequency of free undamped oscillations,

— attenuation coefficient,

— cyclic frequency of free damped oscillations,

— period of oscillation,

— oscillation amplitude at the time equal to the period,

— logarithmic decrement,

— energy at the start of the experiment,

— current displacement,
